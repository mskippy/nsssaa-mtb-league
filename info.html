Race 5 - Frome Enduro Race Info

We are back on the North Shore this week (Wednesday, May 7th) racing on Fromme. Here is the info that you need to know...

  • Registration / Chip Pick Up 2pm - at the water towers - please do not drop off riders here as there will be limited space at the top.
  • Parking - The parking lot will get full fast. Please DO NOT park on Fromme road, leading up to the parking. You can park along Coleman. You can bike the road, or there is a climb trail called "King of the Shore" that can also get you to the parking lot, please carpool or get dropped off
  • Warm-up laps - you are encouraged to warm up before climbing for your races - you can warm up on Bobsled or Floppy
  • Rider's Briefing - 2:50(ish) - water towers. Sr/Jr Riders near the gate, Juv/Bant further back
  • Start Climb - 3:00 pm - Sr/Jr's will start the climb in waves. Once they have all passed Bobsled, then Juv/Bant riders will start the climb in waves.

    • Everyone will ride back from Expresso/Lower Expresso via the Baden Powell and NOT the road. You will only be on the road when get to the end of BP and need to climb back to the water towers.

    Don't forget to return your chips!!!

Race 4 Info - Alice Lake Enduro

Location: Alice Lake Park

Registration: Floating Dock Picknic area

Race 4 Notes: IMPORTANT

  • Rider debrief at 2:45 pm at the registration area
  • Riders will be relased in waves starting at 3:00 pm (if your team is late, no worries, we'll send you out in a later wave)
  • BE RESPECTFUL OF SIGNATE AND YOUR BEHAVIOUR there were several complaints regarding poor rider behaviour at our race last week. Please be mindful of the following
    • Riding is restricted to the road and trails designated for Riding
    • When riding on the road, be mindful of cars and pedestrians and DO NOT take up the width of the road (The road is not your personal area to show off your precieved bike skills)
    • Bikes are not to be ridden down to the beach
    • Do not harrass the wildlife

      • Poor behaviour reflects badly on you, your team, and the mountain biking community

      Repeat of these poor behaviours will jepoardize future use of this site

      It is very disappointing that the above had to be said, and that the beviours of a few riders, will affect the whole league - we can do better.

Race 3 Info - Alice Lake XC

Location: Alice Lake Park

Registration: Will be at the south end of the lake, by the floating dock area.

Parking: Floating Dock Picknic area Please try to carpool to Alice Lake as well be mindful that there are no large buses allowed in this area. Smaller buses are fine, but large buses will need to park by the pump track / paddleboard rental area.

Washrooms: Please use the available facilities to go to the washrooms. Anyone who doesn't, jepordizes our permit for future races.

Do not use the facili-TREES, use the facilities!

Course Info: April 23 - Squamish High School XC

  • Alice Lake Access -start
  • Up Rob's Corners
  • Down Cardrona (loop here for Jr/Sr)
  • Miki's Magic Part 2
  • Cliff's Corners

Race Notes
